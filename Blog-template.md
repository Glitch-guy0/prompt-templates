## Blog flow structure

final blog structure flow
Subject heading
Sub heading
What we are going to cover
Tldr (50 words, detailed tldr will be in footer)
introduction before moving on (this will have all tldr explanation of prerequisites)
Problem statement
Solution
Step by step instruction to solution
Next problem (if exists)
Solution
Step by step instruction and explanation
this problem statement => solution => step by step explanation or instruction continues if the blog holds it.
detailed tldr covering star of the show which developers love to check fast when they just need to read and move on with their lives
footer explaining how i got to this stage and exploration and how this blog came to existence a lite heart fun section if user is reading this blog and get to know author personal side of things which must relatable to this blog content.
