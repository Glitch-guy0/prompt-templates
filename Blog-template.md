# Blog Structure Template

## Content Architecture

This template provides a proven structure for technical blog posts that balance depth with accessibility.

---

## Part 1: Opening Section

### Subject Heading
Main topic title that captures the core concept.

### Sub Heading
More specific angle or context for the subject.

### Coverage Overview
Brief outline of what readers will learn and key topics covered.

### TLDR (50 words)
Concise summary placed prominently for scanners. *Detailed expansion of key points will be in the footer.*

### Introduction
- Explain prerequisites and foundational context
- Elaborate on the TLDR points
- Set expectations for what follows

---

## Part 2: Problem-Solution Flow

Follow this pattern for each problem in your post:

### Problem Statement
Clearly articulate the challenge or question being addressed.

### Solution
Present the core solution or approach.

### Step-by-Step Instructions
Provide detailed, actionable steps to implement the solution.

### Next Problem (if applicable)
If multiple related problems exist, repeat the flow:
1. Problem Statement
2. Solution  
3. Step-by-Step Instructions & Explanations

---

## Part 3: Closing Section

### Developer's Quick Reference
A detailed TLDR highlighting the "star of the show" — what developers love to grab quickly when they just need key takeaways.

### Footer: The Behind-the-Scenes
Share:
- How you arrived at this solution (exploration process)
- Key discoveries or pivots along the way
- Personal insights related to the content
- Relatable, lighthearted reflections that humanize the post and connect with readers
